ℹ️ About NagrikVaani

India's voice-first public grievance portal, built as a student prototype project.

Our Mission

NagrikVaani (Citizen Voice) is built to democratize access to government grievance redressal. By enabling voice-based complaint filing in English, Hindi, and Marathi, we empower citizens who face literacy barriers, ensuring no grievance goes unheard.

🎙️

Voice-First

File complaints in your mother tongue without typing a single word.

🌐

Multilingual

Auto-translates Hindi and Marathi to English for processing.

🧠

Smart Routing

NLP keyword detection auto-categorizes and routes complaints.

🔍

Easy Tracking

Track your complaint status anytime using your unique ID.
